# Video Prompt: Catholic / Western Civilization TikTok Edit

## Overview

Create a short cinematic vertical video (9:16 format) designed for TikTok or Instagram Reels.
The video should feel reverent, historical, and civilizational, highlighting the role of the
Catholic Church and the Traditional Latin Mass in shaping Western civilization.

- **Format:** 9:16 vertical (1080 × 1920px)
- **Duration:** 20–25 seconds
- **Frame rate:** 30fps
- **Tone:** Reverent, solemn, inspiring

---

## Visual Style

- Dark, moody lighting
- Golden candlelight tones
- Incense smoke
- Slow camera movement (prefer footage with slow pans or static shots)
- Dramatic cathedral interiors
- Ancient and historical imagery

**Evoke:**
- Sacred tradition
- Continuity across centuries
- The beauty of Catholic worship
- The cultural roots of Western civilization

**Avoid:** modern graphics, flashy transitions, quick cuts, neon colors, or anything that looks contemporary.

---

## Music

**Type:** Gregorian chant  
**Preferred tracks (in order of preference):**
1. Dies Irae
2. Kyrie Eleison
3. Salve Regina
4. Adoro Te Devote

**Audio behavior:**
- Fade in quietly for first 3 seconds
- Slowly build in presence (not volume) toward middle
- Fade out gently over final 3 seconds
- Solemn and atmospheric — NOT cinematic trailer music

---

## Pacing

| Element | Specification |
|---|---|
| Clip length | 2–3 seconds per clip |
| Transitions | Crossfade / slow dissolve, 0.4s |
| Total clips | 7–10 |
| Total length | 20–25 seconds |

No quick cuts. Every transition should feel unhurried.

---

## Scene List

### Scene 1 — Cathedral Interior
**Footage:** Gothic cathedral interior with candlelight
**Text:** *"For over 1,500 years…"*
**Duration:** 2.5s

### Scene 2 — Latin Mass
**Footage:** Priest celebrating Mass, ideally ad orientem (facing altar), or elevating the host
**Text:** *"This was the Mass of the West."*
**Duration:** 3s

### Scene 3 — Incense
**Footage:** Incense rising around the altar, smoke catching the light
**Text:** *"Monks prayed it."*
**Duration:** 2.5s

### Scene 4 — Monastic Life
**Footage:** Monks in choir stalls, monks processing through cloisters, or a monastery library
**Text:** *"Saints worshiped this way."*
**Duration:** 2.5s

### Scene 5 — Saints / Names
**Footage:** Statues or paintings of saints, medieval Christian art
**Text:** Names appear one at a time:
- *Augustine*
- *Thomas Aquinas*
- *Joan of Arc*
- *Padre Pio*

**Duration:** ~0.7s each (cycling through the same or similar clip)

### Scene 6 — Architecture
**Footage:** Stained glass windows with sunlight, cathedral nave, stone abbey exterior
**Text:** *(no text, visual breathing room)*
**Duration:** 2.5s

### Scene 7 — Illuminated Manuscript
**Footage:** Close-up of illuminated manuscript pages, or a monastic scriptorium
**Text:** *(no text)*
**Duration:** 2s

### Final Scene — Altar / Host
**Footage:** Altar candles glowing in a cathedral, or priest elevating the host, or incense in golden light
**Text (large, centered, gold):**
```
Lex Orandi
Lex Credendi
```
**Text (small, below, white):**
```
The Mass that built the West.
```
**Duration:** 3.5s (hold text before fade out)

## Color Grade

Apply to all clips:

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Red channel | +12% (warmth) |
| Blue channel | −12% (remove cool tones) |
| Shadow crush | Gamma 1.15 (slightly darker shadows) |
| Film grain | Subtle (std dev ~6) |
| Vignette | Soft radial darkening at edges |
| Contrast | Slightly increased |

Result should feel: **ancient, warm, timeless** — like a historical documentary or monastery footage.

---

## Typography

**Preferred fonts (in order):**
1. Cinzel
2. Trajan Pro
3. Garamond
4. Times New Roman

**Text style:**
- Color: white (#FFFFFF) for most text; gold (#D4AF37) for final Lex Orandi card
- Large, centered, minimal
- Fade in over 0.3s — hold — fade out over 0.3s
- Drop shadow: 2px offset, black, semi-transparent

---

## Emotional Goal

The viewer should feel:
- **Reverence** — this is sacred
- **Awe** — the Church built something extraordinary
- **Historical continuity** — this has endured for 1,500 years
- **Cultural heritage** — this shaped the West

The video should communicate that the Traditional Latin Mass was not a relic — it was the living center of Western civilization for over a millennium.
